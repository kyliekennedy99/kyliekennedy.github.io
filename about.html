Hi, I’m Kylie Kennedy—a recent graduate of Computer Science student from UW–Madison. I build fast, delightful full-stack apps (React/TypeScript, Python/Node, SQL), mentor new coders, and love turning messy data into simple, usable UX.

Lately I’ve been exploring serverless, event-driven systems for real-time collaboration and distributed reliability. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me running the Lakeshore Path, sketching UI ideas in Procreate, or paddling on Lake Mendota—and cheering on the Badgers at Camp Randall.
