Hi, I’m Kylie—a fourth-year Computer Science student at UW-Madison who loves building delightful full-stack apps, mentoring new coders, and spending Saturdays paddling on Lake Mendota. My research interests revolve around data-driven UX and distributed systems, and I’m currently exploring serverless architectures for real-time collaboration tools.

Beyond the terminal you’ll find me running the Lakeshore Path, sketching UI mock-ups in Procreate, or cheering on the Badgers at Camp Randall.
